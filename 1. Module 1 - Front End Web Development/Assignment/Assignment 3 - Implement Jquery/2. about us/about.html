What is ABC Learning Center

ABC Learning Center is a institute engaged in non-formal education, primarily focusing on programming education. This company was founded in 2019 in Indonesia. ABC Learning Center develops various technology-based learning services, including virtual classroom services, online exam platforms, subscription learning videos, and other educational content that can be accessed via the web.

Our Vision

We want to provide technology-based educational services to help you realize your dreams.

Our Mission

Provide friendly and quality service. Provide the latest and exciting content. Prepare a professional mentor so that it is easier for you to achieve your dreams.
